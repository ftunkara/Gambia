About Me

Welcome to My Gambia Website!

Hello! I'm Fatoumata Tunkara, a West-African American student passionate about sharing the beauty and culture of The Gambia with the world. This website is my personal project to showcase the rich heritage, diverse people, and amazing destinations of my beloved country.

My Story

I tell my experiences through stories relating to my time back in Africa. During the couple of years I spent there, I realized many cultural differences between Africa and America. Additionally, I learned many meaningful themes that I continue to apply to my life on a daily basis.

Although I am viewed as quiet by my peers, my writing hints at the details of what I have gone through in my experience of the homeland. Through this website, I share those experiences and insights with others who are curious about The Gambia and West African culture.

My Connection to The Gambia

I have deep roots in The Gambia and wanted to create a comprehensive resource that captures the essence of what makes this country so special. From the bustling markets of Banjul to the peaceful villages like Allunhare, every corner of The Gambia has a story to tell.

What This Website Offers

  • Cultural Insights: Explore traditional customs, music, and celebrations
  • Destination Guides: Discover the unique character of different regions
  • People & Communities: Learn about the diverse ethnic groups that call The Gambia home
  • Fun Facts: Discover interesting tidbits about this fascinating country
  • Personal Experiences: Stories and insights from my time in The Gambia

My Mission

Through this website, I hope to inspire others to learn about and appreciate The Gambia's rich cultural heritage. Whether you're planning a visit or simply curious about this beautiful country, I want to share the warmth and hospitality that Gambians are known for, along with the valuable lessons I learned during my time there.

Fatoumata Tunkara
