Allunhare

Allunhare is a picturesque village in The Gambia, known for its peaceful atmosphere, traditional way of life, and close-knit community. It offers visitors a glimpse into rural Gambian culture and hospitality. Life there is largely driven by subsistence farming and family-based economies.
